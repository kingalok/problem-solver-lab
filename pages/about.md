<section class="post-article">
  <p>I am Alok (kingalok), a technology architect who enjoys building systems that reduce chaos: automation that saves hours, architectures that scale safely, and tools that turn ideas into repeatable outcomes.</p>

  <h2>What you will find here</h2>
  <ul>
    <li>Practical playbooks for cloud, DevOps, and platform engineering</li>
    <li>AI and automation experiments (agents, workflows, integrations)</li>
    <li>Architecture blueprints and decision frameworks</li>
    <li>Templates you can copy (repos, pipelines, checklists, docs)</li>
    <li>Case studies: what worked, what did not, and why</li>
  </ul>

  <h2>How I work</h2>
  <p>I follow a simple loop: Frame -> Build -> Test -> Ship -> Learn -> Repeat.</p>
  <p>I care more about clear thinking and measurable results than fancy complexity.</p>

  <h2>What I am optimizing for</h2>
  <ul>
    <li>Leverage: build once, reuse many times</li>
    <li>Compounding: small improvements that stack over months</li>
    <li>Clarity: explain it so a smart person can execute fast</li>
    <li>Truth over hype: show evidence, tradeoffs, and constraints</li>
  </ul>

  <h2>Who this is for</h2>
  <p>If you are a builder, architect, engineer, or founder who likes clean solutions and repeatable systems, you will feel at home here.</p>
  <p>If something helps you, feel free to open an issue, suggest an improvement, or collaborate on a better version.</p>
</section>
